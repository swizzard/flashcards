Bengali Flashcards

how-to

clicking on the card flips from front to back

incorrect will move the card to the back of the stack

correct will remove the card from the stack

what those words mean to you otherwise is up to you

when the stack is emtpy, it will reset

about

sam raker made this website to help him learn বাংলা (bangla/bengali)

bangla vocabulary, romanization, and english translation are all taken from Complete Bengali by William Radice. no copyright is claimed.

as of march 21, 2025 the list contains all words through unit 7, plus a few extras.

d.i.y.

it's just html, css, and vanilla js

fork the repo and replace the front and back of the objects in cards.mjs (you'll probably want to change the title too)

host your own flashcards on netlify vel sim., or you can run it locally with static-file-http-server vel sim.
